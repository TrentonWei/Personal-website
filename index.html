Zhiwei Wei (魏智威)

PhD

Wuhan University

assistant professor

The Aerospace Information Research Institute, Chinese Academic of Sciences

Currently, I'm working as an Assistant Professor in The Aerospace Information Research Institute, Chinese Academic of Sciences. My research interests including (1)Technologies for Creating Explanatory and Exploratory Animations for Spatial Data; (2) Automated Understanding and Processing of Spatial Data.

I received a PhD degree at the Wuhan University during Sep.2015 - Jul.2020, advised by Prof. Qingsheng Guo. My main area of interest is automate map generalization, especially the progressive generalization of building maps. I finished my doctor thesis as "Research on Progressive Generalization for Building Maps with Spatial Distribution (顾及空间分布特征的建筑物群渐进式综合研究)"

I received my B.Sc and majored in GIS at the Wuhan University during Sep.2011 - Jul.2015. I finished my bachelor thesis as 'Computation on spatial characteristics of buildings(建筑物图形的空间特征计算方法)', and honored as the Excellent Bachelor Thesis of Hubei Province .
